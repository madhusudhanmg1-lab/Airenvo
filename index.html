The Problem

Air pollution contributes to over 7 million premature deaths globally each year, while plastic waste continues to exceed 400 million tons annually. Urban environments today face rising emissions and declining indoor air quality.

The Solution

Airenvo is building integrated environmental solutions led by EcoPulse Tree. We focus on connected, scalable innovations like compostable packaging and moss curtains to address multiple challenges together.

Our Vision

We envision a future where sustainability is not approached through isolated fixes, but through connected systems that actively improve environmental performance.
